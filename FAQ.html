Frequently Asked Questions

We have no doubt that many individuals and businesses are able to do their own accounting, but with Accuracy you can free up hours of your schedule. Accounting is a long process, complicated and sometimes aggravating. Accuracy can take that pain off your hands, and become a wonderful business partner in the process!

Our employees can perform any accounting service you might need. We have bookkeepers, accountants, and CPAs. Visit the Customer Service web page to see pricing.

Absolutely! Call our office from 8:00 A.M. to 7:00 P.M. to schedule an appointment. Visit the Contact Us page for information.

Are you registered with the IRS?

Absolutely. Our firm is certified by the IRS.

Can I contact you after tax season?

Of course! Our services can be employed any time of year. Just give us a call!
