Customer Service

At Accuracy, we pride ourselves in helping our customers however we can. If you have a problem with our services, complaints or concerns regarding our company, please let us know right away. We have 24/7 support on the line 365 days a year. Feel free to leave an e-mail or send us a letter at any time. For our contact information, please visit the "contact us" option in the menu at the top of the screen. For more common questions, visit the "FAQ" section instead.

Pricing/Rates

Accuracy prides itself in low rates for your accounting needs. Our employees require pay by the hour, but can be completely customized for your needs, whether you're a single account, a small business, or even an enterprise.

  • Bookkeeping - $25-$35 per hour
    • Our Bookkeeping staff can help you with all of your organization needs.
  • Accounting - $35 - $50 per hour
    • Our accountants can assist you with budgeting and keeping your account balanced.
  • CPA (Certified Public Accountant) - $100+ per hour
    • Our CPA staff is certified to perform anything from accounting to complex transactions through your account. Highly trained professionals, the higher cost is absolutely worthwhile
      for these versatile employees.
